الصفات (Adjectives)

طيب (ṭayyib) = kind, good-hearted, nice (masc.)

طيبة (ṭayyiba) = kind (fem.)

طيبون (ṭayyibūn) = kind (masc. plural)

طيبات (ṭayyibāt) = kind (fem. plural)

نساء قويات = strong women

قوة (quwwa) = “strength” (a noun, not an adjective).

What you need is the adjective قوي (qawī) = strong.

Its plural form is أقوياء (aqwiyāʾ).

رجل قوي = a strong man

رجال أقوياء = strong men

امرأة قوية = a strong woman

نساء قويات = strong women
